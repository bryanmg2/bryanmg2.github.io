Where is my money going?

These 5 institutions of higher education in Oregon are legitimate schools that confer bachelor's degrees and invest more money in their students' education than they receive in revenue from the students.

  • Oregon Health & Science University
  • Oregon Institute of Technology
  • Portland State University
  • Southern Oregon University
  • Western Oregon University
To answer Steve Jobs' question, "is it worth it" for working-class parents to send their kids to these schools? Unequivocally, yes: The State of Oregon, despite its meager resources, is essentially paying people to get degrees at these institutions as an investment in the State's future success.

In the case of OHSU, Oregon invests almost $50,000/yr. per Student for Oregonians to go to medical school, study Bioinformatics, or get a degree in a field related to Healthcare. That's an amazing benefit no matter what State you live in.
